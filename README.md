# 🚗 Automotive Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

> An interactive Business Intelligence dashboard built in **Power BI** to analyze automotive sales performance, revenue trends, customer insights, and regional sales using dynamic visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Automotive Sales Analytics Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of vehicle sales performance. It enables businesses to monitor key sales metrics, identify top-performing brands and models, analyze regional sales trends, and make data-driven decisions through interactive reports.

This project demonstrates my skills in **Power BI, Data Analysis, Data Modeling, DAX, and Business Intelligence**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall vehicle sales performance.
- Monitor monthly and yearly sales trends.
- Identify top-selling car brands and models.
- Compare sales across different regions.
- Evaluate dealer performance.
- Provide interactive business insights for decision-making.
